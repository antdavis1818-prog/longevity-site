Meet Your Home Steward
Built on a lifetime of caring for others.
100% Committed to
Your Family
"This life has taken me down a lot of different paths — but looking back, the plan was always the same. It's always been about helping others."

My name is Anthony Davis, and helping people has been my calling from day one. Growing up close to my grandmother gave me a deep respect and love for the elderly — and knowing what it feels like to need support and not have it has shaped everything I do.

In my earlier years I worked with youth, guiding young people through some of life's hardest challenges. With a degree in psychology, I've always understood that real help goes beyond the practical — it's about dignity, trust, and genuine human connection.

Longevity Property Services was born from that lifelong passion. I wanted to change the narrative around what it looks and feels like to receive help — to show that compassionate, reliable support from a real person who truly cares is possible. For the seniors of Southern New Hampshire and Massachusetts, and the families who love them, that's exactly what I'm here to provide.

Helping Hands Services
A little extra help, when it matters most.

Sometimes the best support isn't about the home itself — it's about the person living in it. Our Helping Hands services offer flexible, friendly assistance for everyday needs that make a real difference in a senior's quality of life.

🛒

Grocery Shopping & Errands

We handle grocery runs, pharmacy pickups, and everyday errands so your loved one doesn't have to worry about getting out — or going without.

📦

Light Moving & Rearranging

Need furniture shifted, boxes moved, or a room reorganized for safety and comfort? We handle the heavy lifting so no one gets hurt trying to do it alone.

🎲

Companionship & Social Visits

Sometimes seniors just need good company. We'll sit down for a game of cards, a puzzle, or a friendly conversation — because connection matters as much as care.

📱

Tech Help

Trouble with the TV remote, a smartphone, or a tablet? We patiently walk seniors through their devices so they can stay connected with family and the world.

Simple, transparent hourly pricing

No contracts required for Helping Hands services. Book as little as one hour at a time. Rates may vary based on location and the nature of the request. Available as a standalone service or as an add-on to any monthly plan.
